biography

currently a 11th grade student at jericho high school in new york, adrian zhang has already earned considerable recognition as a composer.

adrian was accepted into the buti young artists program this year. he was a second-place winner for the 2025 nfmc class 3 composing competition, a finalist in the 2023 national young composers challenge, and an emerging composers prize winner for the 2023 tribeca new music young composers competition. adrian was the mtna 2023 winner of the junior category eastern division. his piano quartet "war" premiered at the "in bloom" concert in new york city in 2023.

adrian began his composing journey improvising on the piano; now, he learns composition from dr. lu wang. adrian aims to compose music that explores both classical and modern styles, creating ambient landscapes in which contrasting elements guide the listener through shifting perspectives.

compositions

a catalog of original works spanning solo piano, chamber music, and orchestral writing.

war
c. 2023

a chamber work in three movements exploring conflict, tension, and resolution. premiered at the "in bloom" concert in new york city. the opening movement employs dense chromatic clusters against a fragmented melodic line, while the finale dissolves into a sustained, luminous chorale.

instrumentation: piano, violin, viola, cello
progression
c. 2023

a solo piano work tracing a continuous arc of harmonic and emotional development. draws on late romantic gesture while incorporating contemporary voice-leading techniques. the piece unfolds as a single unbroken breath, never returning to where it began.

instrumentation: solo piano
quest
c. 2022

an introspective solo piano work built around a searching, modal theme that transforms across a series of variations. each variation strips away or adds layers, revealing different facets of the same underlying idea. the work ends unresolved, mid-thought.

instrumentation: solo piano
