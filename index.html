About Me

Hi! I'm a high school student with a deep passion for understanding the human mind and expressing emotions through music. I'm fascinated by psychology and plan to pursue it in college, while continuing to develop my skills as a classical music composer.

When I'm not studying or composing, you'll find me on the volleyball court playing for my club travel team. I love the teamwork, discipline, and competitive spirit that volleyball brings to my life. Balancing academics, music, and athletics has taught me time management, dedication, and the importance of pursuing what you're passionate about.
